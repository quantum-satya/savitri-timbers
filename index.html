Our Story

Savitri Timbers was founded on a simple belief: quality products and enduring business relationships are built through trust, commitment, and craftsmanship.

Drawing on international sourcing experience and deep industry knowledge, we connect premium timber resources from North America, Europe, Africa, and Latin America with value-added manufacturing capabilities in India.

Our manufacturing base in Kanchanpura, Rajasthan, reflects our commitment to combining global sourcing expertise with local craftsmanship and modern production standards.

Today, we are developing a modern flooring manufacturing facility while expanding our global sourcing network, creating a bridge between the world's forests and customers who value quality, reliability, and long-term partnerships.
