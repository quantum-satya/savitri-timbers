Selected Timber Highlights

Teak

Origin: Benin · Togo · Ivory Coast

Renowned for durability, moisture resistance, and timeless appeal.

Applications: Luxury interiors · Outdoor furniture · Marine use

White Oak

Origin: Europe · North America

Known for exceptional strength, beautiful grain patterns, and versatility.

Applications: Flooring · Furniture · Joinery

White Ash

Origin: USA · Canada

Appreciated for workability, shock resistance, and elegant appearance.

Applications: Furniture · Millwork · Sports equipment

Steam Beech

Origin: Germany · Croatia · Romania

Uniform texture and excellent machining characteristics make it highly versatile.

Applications: Furniture · Panels · Bentwood products

Maple

Origin: USA · Canada

A premium hardwood valued for its fine texture and light colour.

Applications: Cabinetry · Flooring · Decorative interiors

Southern Yellow Pine

Origin: USA

Strong, economical, and highly adaptable.

Applications: Construction · Packaging · Industrial use
