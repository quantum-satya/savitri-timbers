# Purpose

This document defines Savitri Timbers' business positioning.

All marketing content, website copy, brochures, presentations and AI-generated content must follow this document.

If any conflict exists between marketing copy and this document, this document is the source of truth.

---

# Who We Are

Savitri Timbers Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian importer of premium hardwood timber and manufacturer of high-performance wooden sports flooring.

Our business combines trusted international sourcing with precision Indian manufacturing.

---

# Vision

To become India's most trusted supplier of premium hardwoods and sports flooring solutions.

---

# Mission

Deliver premium timber through transparent sourcing, dependable quality and long-term customer relationships.

---

# Core Positioning

Trusted Global Sourcing.

Precision Indian Manufacturing.

---

# Our Competitive Advantage

Our strongest competitive advantage is not price.

Our strongest competitive advantage is trusted sourcing.

Especially for Canadian Maple, we maintain direct supplier relationships that allow us to supply authentic, correctly graded material with greater confidence than traditional trading channels.

---

# Brand Promise

Delivering Quality.

Honouring Commitment.

Every shipment should reflect this promise.

---

# Positioning Statement

Savitri Timbers combines trusted international sourcing with Indian manufacturing expertise to deliver premium hardwood solutions for flooring, sports infrastructure and architectural applications.

---

# Approved Messaging Themes

• Trust

• Authenticity

• Long-term relationships

• Global sourcing

• Manufacturing excellence

• Transparency

• Premium quality

---

# Messaging to Avoid

Avoid:

Lowest Price

Cheapest

Discount

Best in India

World No.1

Guaranteed Best

Unverifiable superlatives.

Always communicate confidence without exaggeration.

---

# AI Rules

AI must always position Savitri Timbers as:

Importer of premium hardwood timber

Manufacturer of sports flooring

Long-term sourcing partner

Premium brand

Never describe Savitri Timbers as an imported flooring company.
