This glossary defines the official business and technical terminology used throughout the Savitri Timbers website and project documentation.

All contributors, including AI assistants, should use these terms consistently.

---

# A

## Architect

A professional involved in planning and designing residential, commercial, institutional, or sports infrastructure projects.

---

# B

## Brand Identity

The visual and communication standards representing Savitri Timbers, including colors, typography, tone of voice, and design language.

---

## Builder

A company or individual responsible for construction projects and a key customer segment for Savitri Timbers.

---

# C

## Customer Solution

A complete offering combining timber sourcing, manufacturing, product supply, and technical support to meet customer requirements.

---

# D

## Dealer

A business that purchases timber or flooring products for resale or distribution.

---

# F

## Featured Business Unit

A strategically highlighted business offering displayed on the homepage to attract visitor attention.

Example:

- Savitri Sports Flooring

---

## Flooring

Finished wooden flooring manufactured by Savitri Timbers using carefully sourced premium timber.

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, the term "flooring" refers to finished products manufactured in India.

---

# G

## Global Timber Sourcing

The process of identifying, evaluating, and procuring premium timber from trusted international suppliers.

---

# I

## Import

The procurement of premium timber (raw material) from international suppliers.

Important:

The company imports timber, **not finished wooden flooring**.

---

## Interior Designer

A professional responsible for interior planning and another primary customer segment.

---

# M

## Manufacturing

The process of converting imported timber into finished wooden flooring products using modern manufacturing techniques.

---

## Maple

One of the approved timber species currently promoted on the website.

---

# P

## Pine

One of the approved timber species currently promoted on the website.

---

## Premium

Represents the company's positioning.

Premium means:

- High quality
- Reliable
- Professional
- Durable
- Trustworthy

It does not simply mean expensive.

---

## Product Portfolio

The collection of products currently offered by Savitri Timbers.

Current portfolio:

- Pine
- Maple
- Teak

---

# Q

## Quality Assurance

Processes followed to ensure timber and flooring meet the company's quality standards before delivery.

---

# R

## Responsible Sourcing

Procuring timber from trusted suppliers while maintaining ethical and sustainable business practices.

---

# S

## Semantic HTML

Writing HTML using meaningful elements such as:

- header
- nav
- main
- section
- article
- footer

This improves accessibility and SEO.

---

## Services

Business capabilities offered by Savitri Timbers beyond products.

Examples:

- Timber Sourcing
- Timber Supply
- Sports Flooring
- Technical Consultation (future)
- Installation Support (future)

---

## Sports Flooring

High-quality wooden flooring systems manufactured by Savitri Timbers for indoor sports facilities.

Typical applications include:

- Basketball Courts
- Badminton Courts
- Indoor Sports Halls
- Educational Institutions
- Sports Complexes

---

## Sports Flooring Solutions

The complete offering associated with sports flooring, including manufacturing, supply, and project support.

---

## Sustainable Growth

Growing the business responsibly while maintaining quality, customer satisfaction, and long-term relationships.

---

# T

## Teak

One of the approved timber species currently promoted on the website.

---

## Timber

Raw wood imported from trusted international suppliers for further processing and manufacturing.

Timber is **not** a finished flooring product.

---

## Timber Sourcing

The process of selecting and procuring premium timber from global suppliers.

---

## Trust

A core value of Savitri Timbers.

The website should reinforce trust through:

- Honest communication
- Professional presentation
- Accurate information
- Reliable commitments

---

# U

## User Experience (UX)

The overall experience visitors have while interacting with the website.

Every design decision should improve:

- Clarity
- Simplicity
- Navigation
- Readability
- Lead generation

---

# V

## Value Addition

The process of transforming imported timber into premium finished products through manufacturing and craftsmanship.

---

## Version

A numbered software release of the website.

Examples:

- Version 1.0
- Version 1.1
- Version 1.2

---

# W

## Wooden Flooring

Finished flooring manufactured by Savitri Timbers from imported premium timber.

Do not describe wooden flooring as an imported product unless explicitly approved.

---

# Website

The official online presence of Savitri Timbers.

Production URL:

https://savitriagro.com

---

# Writing Style

All website content should follow these principles:

- Professional
- Clear
- Accurate
- Trustworthy
- Premium
- Customer-focused

Avoid:

- Exaggeration
- Buzzwords
- Unsupported claims
- Technical jargon where unnecessary
